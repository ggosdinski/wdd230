Membership Benefits

Our Chamber of Commerce offers various memberships tailored to support and enhance the growth of your business, providing access to a wide range of benefits that suit your business needs. Our membership tiers are strategically designed to offer advantages that will drive your company towards success.

  • Basic Membership (NP Membership)
    Cost: Free
    Ideal for non-profit organizations, this membership provides access to our essential resources and fundamental services, allowing you to connect with a valuable network of contacts.
  • Bronze Membership
    Cost: $1050 Argentine pesos (monthly/annual)
    Enjoy exclusive events, specialized training, and prominent visibility on our website. Access privileged discounts on events and training programs.
  • Silver Membership
    Cost: $1500 Argentine pesos (monthly/annual)
    Boost your market presence through prominent advertising on our digital platform. Gain increased discounts on events and exclusive networking opportunities.
  • Gold Membership
    Cost: $2000 Argentine pesos (monthly/annual)
    Preferential access to elite events, premium advertising benefits, and prominent promotion within our partner network. Exclusive discounts on training programs and select services.

Our membership programs are designed to offer a range of benefits that align with your business goals, providing tools, growth opportunities, and visibility that can drive your company's development.
