Demographics Information

Buenos Aires, the capital of Argentina, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse population, and lively neighborhoods. With a population of over 15 million in its metropolitan area, Buenos Aires is one of South America's largest cities. The city's demographics encompass a blend of ethnicities, cultures, and languages, contributing to its unique identity.

Attractions in Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires offers a multitude of attractions for visitors. From the iconic Obelisco and Plaza de Mayo to the historic neighborhoods of San Telmo and La Boca, there's no shortage of places to explore. The city is also renowned for its tango shows, vibrant street art, and thriving culinary scene featuring delicious Argentine cuisine.

Statistics of Buenos Aires

As of the latest statistics, Buenos Aires boasts a diverse economy with strengths in finance, commerce, and tourism. The city's GDP ranks among the highest in South America, driven by sectors such as technology, entertainment, and agriculture. Its strategic location and robust infrastructure make it a key player in the region's economic landscape.
