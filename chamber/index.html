Welcome to the Chamber of Commerce of Buenos Aires

Established over a century ago, we're dedicated to fostering business growth and opportunity in Buenos Aires. Our mission is to empower businesses through advocacy, education, and collaboration.

Throughout our history, we've:

  • Advocated for policies that facilitate trade and business operations.
  • Facilitated networking events resulting in strategic alliances and partnerships.
  • Nurtured startups and provided essential resources for their growth.
  • Championed sustainability and corporate social responsibility initiatives.

Looking forward, our vision is to:

  • Strengthen advocacy efforts for innovation and competitiveness.
  • Expand educational programs tailored for businesses in a dynamic market.
  • Enhance networking opportunities and global partnerships.
  • Continue driving social impact initiatives within the community.

Join us in shaping the future of commerce in Buenos Aires. Together, let's build a thriving business community ready for the challenges of tomorrow.
