Site Plan for "The Chamber of Commerce of Buenos Aires, Argentina"

The Chamber of Commerce of Buenos Aires

The name of our site will be "[The Chamber of Commerce of Buenos Aires]" because it represents our location and purpose.

Site Purpose

Our website serves two primary purposes:

  1. Provide information and resources for local businesses and entrepreneurs to promote economic growth.
  2. Offer community members a platform to stay updated on events, news, and local initiatives.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

  • #231651 - Used for titles and text
  • #8EA8C3 - Used for subtitles
  • #CBF7ED - Used to highlight important information
  • #F5B700 - Used for borders of boxes or images
  • #DC0073 - Used for shadows, buttons, etc.

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Roboto: Used for headings and important text.
